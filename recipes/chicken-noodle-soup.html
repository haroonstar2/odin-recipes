Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

This homemade chicken noodle soup is soul-warming and deliciously simple â€” just chicken and noodles. What makes it so good is the homemade roasted chicken stock.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Chicken fat, broth, breast
  • Egg noodles
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large soup pot
  2. Saute the carrot, onion, celery, and thyme
  3. Add chicken fat and salt
  4. Add chicken broth and bring to boil
  5. Add chicken noodles and boil until tender
  6. Add cooked chicken breast and spices
  7. Serve
