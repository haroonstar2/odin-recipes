Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Beef broth
  • Dried thyme
  • Salt and Pepper
  • French Bread
  • Provolone, Swiss, Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Saute onions in the butter and olive oil until translucent
  2. Add beef broth and thyme and season with salt and pepper
  3. Simmer the mixture for 30 minutes
  4. Preheat the oven's broiler
  5. Ladel the soup into bowls with a slice of bread and cheese on top
  6. Broil it for 2 to 3 minutes
  7. Serve
